Little Grapplers

Little Grapplers introduces young children to the fundamentals of Jiu Jitsu through structured play, games, and age-appropriate drilling.

No prior experience needed. Classes are high-energy, positive, and designed to build confidence alongside technique.

Youth BJJ

Youth BJJ teaches real Jiu Jitsu fundamentals to tweens and teenagers in a focused, structured environment.

This program builds the technical foundation students will use for the rest of their BJJ journey — whether they compete or just train for fitness and self-defense.

  • ✓
    Safe, supervised sparring Controlled live practice that builds real skill and mental toughness.

Jiu Jitsu 101

New to BJJ? Start here.

Jiu Jitsu 101 is designed specifically for adults with little or no experience. You'll learn the essential positions, movements, and techniques in a low-pressure environment where questions are always welcome.

Most of our long-term members started in this class. No fitness level required.

Try a Free Class
  • ✓
    Slower pace, more explanation Coaches break down the "why" behind every technique, not just the steps.
  • ✓
    Patient, experienced training partners You'll drill with people who remember being new — and who want to help.
  • ✓
    Core positions covered first Mount, guard, side control, back — the map of BJJ made simple.
  • ✓
    No fitness level required BJJ will get you fit. You don't need to be fit to start.

Competition Team

The Competition Team is for athletes who want to test their Jiu Jitsu under pressure.

Training is more intense, more focused, and built around preparing you to perform on the tournament mat. If you want to compete — this is where you prepare.

You should have foundational BJJ before joining this program. Ask a coach if you're unsure whether you're ready.

Talk to a Coach
  • ✓
    Advanced technique for competition Guard retention, passing chains, leg locks, and tournament-specific setups.
  • ✓
    Tournament strategy & game planning How to approach a bracket, manage time, and play to your strengths.
  • ✓
    Higher-intensity sparring Timed rounds at competition pace — conditioning your body and your mind.
  • ✓
    Event preparation Support for local and regional IBJJF and NAGA competitions.

Kickboxing

Our Kickboxing program teaches practical stand-up striking in a high-energy, structured class.

No prior striking experience needed. A great standalone program or a complement to BJJ training — stand-up and ground game together make a complete fighter.

View Schedule
  • ✓

  • ✓
    Knees & elbows (Muay Thai influence) Close-range tools that make the striking game complete.
  • ✓
    Cardio, coordination & functional fitness One of the most effective ways to build real conditioning.
