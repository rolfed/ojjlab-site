You're All Set!

We'll be in touch soon to confirm your spot. In the meantime, here's everything you need to know about your first class.

What to Expect

  1. 1
    Arrive 10 minutes early

    Check in at the front desk. A coach or team member will greet you, show you around, and answer any questions before class starts. No need to rush.

  2. 2
    Join the warm-up

    Every class starts with movement drills. Follow along at your own pace — no one is watching to judge. The point is to get your body moving.

  3. 3
    Learn the day's technique

    The coach breaks down a technique, then you practice it with a partner at a controlled, learning pace. There's no pressure to get it perfect the first time.

  4. 4
    Live training is optional on day one

    Classes usually end with live sparring. As a first-timer, there is zero pressure to participate. Watch, rest, or ask questions — we'll let you know when you're ready.

What to Bring

  • ✓
    Athletic clothes

    A t-shirt and shorts or athletic pants. You don't need a gi or a uniform for your first class.

  • ✓
    Water bottle

    Class runs about an hour. You'll want water.

  • ✓
    No shoes on the mat

    Leave footwear at the edge of the mat area. Flip-flops or sandals for getting to and from the mat are helpful.

  • ✓
    Nothing else

    We'll tell you exactly what gear to get after class if you decide to continue. Don't buy anything yet.
