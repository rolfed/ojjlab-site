Visiting for the First Time?

We know walking into a martial arts gym can feel awkward. Here's exactly what to do.

  1. 1
    Come in and say hello

    Introduce yourself at the front desk or to any coach on the floor. We'll take it from there.

  2. 2
    Arrive 10 minutes early

    It gives you time to look around and meet a few people before class starts. Walking in during warm-up is fine too — no pressure.

  3. 3
    Tell us it's your first time

    We'll match you with a training partner and walk you through everything at a pace that makes sense for where you are.

  4. 4
    No gear needed

    Wear athletic clothes. We'll tell you what to get after class if you decide to continue. There's no pressure to sign up for anything on day one.
